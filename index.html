Who We Are

A trusted name in supported living across the East Midlands

East Midlands Property Group is dedicated to sourcing, developing and managing properties that serve those who need supported living. We work closely with care providers, local authorities and families to ensure every home we provide is a genuine foundation for a better life.

🌱

Dignity & Respect

Every resident is treated as an individual, with their needs and choices placed at the heart of all we do.

Quality Environments

🤲

Partnership Approach

What We Offer

Our supported living services

Specialist property solutions that meet the diverse needs of vulnerable individuals and their care teams.

🏡

Supported Living Accommodation

Purpose-configured properties providing a safe, supportive home environment for individuals who need help with daily living whilst retaining their independence.

♿

Disability-Adapted Homes

Fully adapted properties with accessibility features including wheelchair access, wet rooms, level entry and specialist equipment to support physical and mobility needs.

👨‍👩‍👦

Young Adults Transitional Housing

Bridging accommodation for young adults moving out of care or education, supporting the transition to independent living with the right level of guidance.

🧠

Complex Needs Provision

Specialist properties suitable for individuals with learning disabilities, autism, mental health needs or complex behavioural requirements.

🤝

Care Provider Partnerships

We work directly with registered care providers and social care commissioners to source suitable properties matched to their client group's needs.

📋

Property Management

Ongoing property management ensuring maintained standards, compliance and a responsive approach to any issues — so care providers can focus on residents.

Who We Support

Serving those who need it most

Our properties are designed and sourced to meet the needs of a wide range of individuals requiring supported living.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Young Adults

Supporting young people aged 18+ who are transitioning from care, education or family settings into their own supported living environment.

🫀

Physical Disabilities

Providing wheelchair-accessible, fully adapted homes for individuals with physical disabilities who need accessible, barrier-free living spaces.

🌈

Learning Disabilities & Autism

Calm, structured, sensory-considered properties suited to individuals with learning disabilities, autism spectrum conditions and related needs.

💚

Mental Health Needs

Safe and stable housing environments that provide a secure base for individuals managing mental health conditions with professional support.

🏥

Acquired Brain Injuries

Appropriate accommodation for individuals living with acquired brain injuries, configured to support cognitive and physical rehabilitation journeys.

🌟

Special Educational Needs

Housing provision for those whose special educational and care needs require a thoughtful, tailored living environment to support their wellbeing.

How It Works
